My name is Sarah Gallardo and I like to make things better.

I am an email and web designer that specializes in responsive email design.

I love email in a way that most people don’t find agreeable. If you tell me that you bought something on the internet I will likely start asking you questions about what brought you to that purchasing decision- and I get a warm glow when you tell me that think geek.com sent you another email with a cool doctor who gadget you had to have. I also find it outrageous that certain mobile apps send me non-responsive emails to illicit me to use their app… I’ve written a few angry emails…
